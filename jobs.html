The most productive place you'll work.

We're a young, energetic team of analytically-minded folks obsessed with focused, creative productivity. Elbii offers competitive pay and a workspace stipend to spend however you like. You'll get to pick which projects to work on and enjoy a flat hierarchical structure that promotes individualism over corporate homogeneity.

Our garage office is located in the heart of downtown Mountain View just a few blocks from Caltrain and VTA stops. When we're feeling adventurous you may even spot us at one of the nearby coffee shops. We regularly attend Bay Area Meetups and host backyard hacker BBQs over interesting discussions and great beer.

We're always looking for smart folks who get things done. We especially like people who take an engineering approach to any given task:
  1. Define the problem
  2. Explore the solution space
  3. Design the solution
  4. Implement the solution
  5. Incorporate feedback
