More Information

Why should you care about security?

Let's put it this way. It's 2014. We live in an age of government backdoors, rampant privacy abuse, and user database leaks nearly everyday. If you don't make user privacy and security a priority, you will eventually get hacked, your users will be pissed, and they'll look to your competitors for services they can trust.

What's considered "secure"?

Your users should be confident their information is safe with you. Today, this means using HTTPS everywhere (perhaps with independently verified certificate signatures), dedicated servers, encrypted storage, 2-factor authentication, and strong password policies. Guess what? We have experience implementing all of that stuff. Don't make the all-too-common mistake of trying to build your own.
